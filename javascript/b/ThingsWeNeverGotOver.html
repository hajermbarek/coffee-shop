Things We Never Got Over

Written by : Lucy Score

Grumpy . Small-town . Steamy

Short Description

Naomi Witt, a runaway bride who has spent her life fixing everyone else's problems, drives into the small town of Knockemout, Virginia to rescue her estranged twin sister from yet another mess. But Tina promptly steals Naomi's car, her money, and abandons her 11-year-old niece—leaving Naomi stranded with a child she never knew existed, no resources, and a dangerously grumpy barber named Knox who reluctantly comes to her aid. As Naomi fights to create a stable life for her niece while dodging her sister's chaos, she discovers that Knox, despite his "don't care" attitude, might be exactly what she needs.
